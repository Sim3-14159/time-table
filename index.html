Terminology:
There are 2 teams: AM and PM, each of which has a clock. On each clock there
is a hand. AM and PM take turns moving their minutes (the red circles) by clicking
their hours (the minutes' enclosing circles).

Playing the Game:
Each team plays, starting with AM. Each turn, a team can move:
  1. the hour that their clock's hand is currently pointing to
  2. the hour that their last minute landed in on their last turn

Remember, though: wherever you click, your opponent's hand will go to that same
position on their clock, and wherever their hand points, that is where they will be
able to click.

Winning:
A team wins by collecting 20 minutes in any one of their hours.
